    Stop Drowning in Form Frustration. Start Winning with Basin.

    Sick of wrestling with clunky form setups? Tired of spam flooding your inbox? Wasting hours trying to connect your forms to other tools? There's a better way.

    Basin is the dead-simple, no-code form backend that lets you set up powerful, reliable forms in under 5 minutes. Seriously.

    Forget complex coding. Forget server headaches. Basin handles the backend grunt work so you can focus on what actually matters: growing your business.

    Why Basin Will Change Your Life (or at Least Your Forms)

    • Launch Forms Lightning Fast: Go from idea to live form in minutes, not days.
    • Crush Spam Instantly: Built-in protection and easy integrations with hCaptcha, Google reCAPTCHA, Cloudflare Turnstile.
    • Connect Everything Seamlessly: Integrate with tools you already use like Zapier, Mailchimp, Google Sheets, Microsoft Teams, Slack and more.
    • Deploy Anywhere: Basin plays nice with your favorite static site generators and hosting platforms: Webflow, Shopify, Wordpress, Netlify, Vercel, Cloudflare Pages, Render, AWS Amplify, Surge, or plain HTML.
    • Developer Friendly, Business Ready: Powerful enough for developers, simple enough for your entire team.
